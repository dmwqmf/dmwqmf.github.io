Meet Destiny Ward

Welcome to Destiny Ward's portfolio website. The purpose of the portfolio is to reflect on the quality of Destiny's work and achievements.

Destiny Ward is a proactive senior at the University of Missouri-Columbia. Destiny is also a thoughtful leader in business technology and digital media design. Since a young age, she has had an interest in engineering. In fact, her first contact with anything related to engineering was putting together a robotic toy machine at the age of 7. She is passionate about learning new skills and creating innovative opportunities in technology. Starting from scratch without any coding or technical skills related to software engineering, Destiny has limb a challenging ladder of STEM to reach the lifestyle and goals of her desire. Destiny's ability to solve real-world problems is what drives her incredible ambition and academic acceleration. She has put her soft skills to the test in combination with her technical skills. The purpose of this website is to share Destiny's successful experience as well adversities she has faced as a student and employee. In Destiny's perspective, goal-setting and consistent progression are key.
